Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 24, 2025

DriveFinder is a Chrome Extension that helps users search and retrieve documents stored in their own Google Drive. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your data.

What We Collect

  • Google Sign-In is used only for authentication.
  • We access your Google Drive file metadata (like file names and types) to perform searches. We do not access file content.

How We Use Your Data

  • We use your data locally to help you search your Google Drive documents.
  • No personal data or file content is stored or shared.

Third-Party Services

We use Google APIs (Drive and Identity) to provide core functionality. These are official APIs provided by Google and comply with their security practices.

Data Sharing

We do not share your data with any third parties.

Data Storage

Preferences like your last search are stored only in your browser (local storage). No data is stored externally.

Security

All communication with Google APIs is encrypted and secured.

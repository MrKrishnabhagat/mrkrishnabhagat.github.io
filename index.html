Frequently Asked Questions

Is DriveFinder free to use?
Yes, DriveFinder is completely free to use with all features included.
Is my data secure with DriveFinder?
Absolutely. DriveFinder uses Google's secure OAuth authentication and never stores your credentials or file data. All searches happen directly between your browser and Google's servers.
Does DriveFinder work with Google Workspace accounts?
Yes, DriveFinder works with both personal Google accounts and Google Workspace (formerly G Suite) accounts.
What permissions does DriveFinder require?
DriveFinder only requires read-only access to your Google Drive files so it can search them. It cannot modify, delete, or share your files.
Can I use DriveFinder offline?
DriveFinder requires an internet connection to search your Google Drive files.

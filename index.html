Install the Extension

Frequently Asked Questions

Is DriveFinder free to use? +
Yes, DriveFinder is completely free to use with all features included. No hidden fees or premium versions - everyone gets the full experience.
Is my data secure with DriveFinder? +
Absolutely. DriveFinder uses Google's secure OAuth authentication and never stores your credentials or file data. All searches happen directly between your browser and Google's servers with industry-standard encryption.
Does DriveFinder work with Google Workspace accounts? +
Yes, DriveFinder works with both personal Google accounts and Google Workspace (formerly G Suite) accounts. It's perfect for businesses of all sizes.
What permissions does DriveFinder require? +
DriveFinder only requires read-only access to your Google Drive files so it can search them. It cannot modify, delete, or share your files, ensuring your documents remain exactly as you left them.
Can I use DriveFinder offline? +
DriveFinder requires an internet connection to search your Google Drive files, as it communicates directly with Google's servers to provide you with the most up-to-date results.
